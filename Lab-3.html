“Even if Cal Poly didn’t score much more at home than away, they still played better. This is because they played better defense at home, so the scores overall were lower.” After the analysis of our opponents teams’ scoring, it seems that this statement is accurate. Although Cal Poly doesn’t score may not score much more, their defense seems to improve a lot. Cal Poly holds their opponent to about 71.3 points instead of the 79 points average for away games. The 71.33 is actually lower than their scoring average, which means in total they are scoring more than they are getting scored on. No team scored 100 on them at home, unlike on the road. This would account for why they win about 20% more home games than away games.
